I am currently attending the Nashville Software School Full-Stack Software Development program.


I am an avid problem solver that loves to create solutions to real world problems with technology. The desire to create applications that allow people to connect, share , and create together keeps me up at night.


I have always held interest in how software worked behind the scenes. After graduating from the University of Memphis, and spending a year working as the head of marketing at the New Daisy Theatre on Beale Street. Throughout my year of working with web applications, pages, and social media, I became determined and inspired to turn to a career path that would satisy my desire to be creative, my love of language, and my fascination with software.
